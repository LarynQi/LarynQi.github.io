Slowify

Play, loop, speed up or slow down music with the official AFX Music App. Upload music from Google Drive or from your phone to get started. Perfect for dancers, musicians, or singers!

AFX is a dance group at UC Berkeley. As computer science students who love dance, AFX Tech Committee built this app to serve not only our dance community at Cal but also dancers and artists all around the world. Features include playing, looping, slowing down/speeding up, visualization, and link sharing with others. The Android app is also designed to be compatible with the website version.

  • Organization: AFX Tech
  • Tech: Android Studio, Java, MediaPlayer API
  • Link: Google Play Store
  • Date: Fall 2019 - Spring 2020
